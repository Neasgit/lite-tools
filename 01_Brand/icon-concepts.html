Where things stand
Strongest picks
A — Filled Corners evolves the Viewfinder wordmark directly. Same language, much more weight. B — Crosshair is more aggressive and tactical — works for true crime. Both hold at 16px.
The square crosshair hybrid
G — Square Crosshair combines both: corner marks + crosshair lines in pure square geometry. Bridges the Viewfinder and Crosshair concepts. Cohesive with the wordmark but can feel busy at small sizes.
Set aside
E — Play Frame feels generic unless paired with corner marks. C, D, F are reference points, not live options.
