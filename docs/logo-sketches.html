Strong wordmark · but the amber bar loses all meaning at small sizes. At 16px it's a yellow stripe, not a brand mark. Needs the LE monogram as a companion.

Why this works

  • The amber bar is already your brand's recurring fingerprint · making it part of the logo reinforces recognition
  • Purely typographic · no separate mark to maintain. Works at any size when paired with the wordmark
  • The bar above "THE EDIT" frames the edit · the thing that was intentionally left in

Use for

  • Channel banner
  • Thumbnail watermark
  • Website header
  • Social bios
  • Merch

The [E] mark is genuinely ownable · editorial brackets are the literal definition of your channel name. Readable to 16px. No other true crime channel uses this symbol.

Why this works

  • Square brackets are the universal symbol for "left in" · editorial notation for content that wasn't removed
  • Documentary and legal connotation · evidence, transcripts, annotations
  • The amber [E] becomes a standalone mark · readable at small sizes and genuinely ownable in the true crime space

Use for

  • Channel art
  • Profile picture ([E] only)
  • Intros / outros
  • Merchandise
  • Favicon

Why this works

  • Creates visual motion and asymmetry · the most dynamic of the five concepts
