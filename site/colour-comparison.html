Locked: Documentary Amber.

Highest contrast against the YouTube grey chrome. Reads as evidence-light / streetlight, not as caution tape. Underused in the bodycam subgenre · most channels go yellow, which signals "the subgenre" not "this channel". Bone was the bravest move but the brand carries restraint elsewhere; trying to do it through colour too left thumbnails feeling empty. Mint and Copper both lost feed visibility. The recognition load is now carried by amber + the v2 timestamp glyph + the LITE mark working together.
